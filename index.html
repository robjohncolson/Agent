Unit 6: Inference for Proportions

Conditions, Assumptions & the p̂ vs p₀ Distinction
The Big Idea: When building a confidence interval, we check the Large Counts condition with p̂ (the sample proportion). When running a significance test, we check it with p₀ (the hypothesized proportion from H₀). Mixing these up is the #1 student error on the AP exam.
